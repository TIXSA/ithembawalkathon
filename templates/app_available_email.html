Registered for the Walkathon and can’t wait for the big day? But while waiting for your big day, you can start having fun with our Walkathon Mobile App.

 

 

 

Keeping abreast of cancer awareness and promoting early detection is at your fingertips now through the iThemba Walkathon Mobile App! Download yours today from your App Store or Play Store and help us promote the Walkathon!

 

 

 

Discover so much more while staying at home including breast health messages that you can share with your loved ones, and the latest news on the Walkathon activities, such as:

·       Social media posters – download and share with your loved ones

·       Videos on breast health – download and share with your loves ones

·       Track your steps and get recognition messages as you reach milestones
